About More About our company and its goals.

Successful organizations understand that business content matters more than ever as mobile, social and cloud technologies transform their business models. They are discovering new value with business content solutions that bring the power of analytics, process optimization and collaboration to all forms of content to achieve greater degrees of insight and customer engagement using various ECM/BPM products.

Managing content and information is one of the most crucial requirements for today’s organizations. Content is clearly defined a king these days when it comes to an efficient operation and gaining a competitive advantage over others. Since unstructured information comprises of over 80% of an organization’s information assets, on average Enterprise Content Management (ECM) emerged as one of the top 4 priorities for a vast majority of CIOs today. Every Industry segment, every organization and every department will have different requirements because of the specificity and varied objectives. Hence it takes an in-depth understanding of industries, companies and business processes in order to offer the most suitable content management solution and to be able to implement the same for maximum ROI

Features. Some of the coolest Features of our service offerings.

Content management and Imaging

Today’s mobile, social and cloud transformation is leading to exponential growth in business content. This places greater demands on organizations to effectively capture and manage this unstructured data for competitive advantage. Content management and imaging solutions from SPS can help companies capture, store, manage, view and deliver various types of content where and when their users need it to move at the speed of business.

  • Capture content at the point of origin. Quickly and effectively capture content from multiple channels by automating the extraction of data from both paper and digital documents wherever and whenever they originate. With near real time classification of this information, you can improve productivity and accelerate business processes to enable faster responses to customers.
  • Access content across your enterprise. Provide unified access to any content type on any device for better decision making with a security-rich, open and scalable content platform. Help ensure that your employees are never at a loss for information with collaborative and mobile document management that can be deployed on premises and in the cloud for increased speed of execution.
  • Engage users to improve customer service and responsiveness. Increase transparency and responsiveness by providing customers content when they need it with high-performance electronic statement retrieval and enterprise report management. Automatically index, store and provide 24x7 self-service access to more quickly resolve inquires and enhance customer retention while gaining print and storage savings.

Advanced case management

Solutions enable decision makers to assemble, analyze, act on and archive content for improved business outcomes. These solutions make it possible to gain value from the wealth of information in documents, data, video, audio and social media. Empower case workers—whether they’re answering customer requests, completing loan applications or adjudicating a claim—by giving them the tools to reach solutions faster and more accurately. Automate the right process, apply the right analysis and involve the right people to create the most impactful workflow for your organization. Advanced case management solutions can help your organization make content processes consistent across business divisions and give decision makers the control and insight they need to resolve cases more effectively. This helps verify access to vital information that leads to better-informed decisions. In addition, SPS advanced case management solutions can help you to:

  • Take advantage of customizable frameworks and reusable templates to provide flexible solutions—with minimal setup.
  • Bring analytics, advanced document search technology and fact-based reporting to case workers.
  • Access and update case files remotely with mobile and cloud-based case solutions.
  • Retain the necessary case information for regulatory or legal requirements with a full audit trail.
  • Experience seamless integration with advanced document capture for rapid input and conversion of key documents.

Information lifecycle governance

Data lifecycle management solutions help you manage your business information throughout its lifecycle, from requirements to retirement. The solutions support a range of application systems, databases and storage media—including big data environments. With lifecycle management, you can derive more value from your data, improve application performance and reduce risk.Data lifecycle management solutions help you:

  • Understand where data resides, what information domains exist and how data is used across the enterprise.
  • Create database structures to enhance testing, productivity and team collaboration.
  • Archive historical data to Hadoop to manage growing amounts of data in product environments.
  • Consolidate and decommission applications that are redundant or no longer current.
  • Maintain data warehouse performance despite growing data volumes.
  • Prevent exposure of confidential data in both production and non-production environments.
